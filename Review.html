My birthday dinner
I went there for my birthday dinner with my girlfriend on November 22 last year, Chef Orng arranged the surprise set menu for us which are included Australian black Angus beef tenderloin, Tartare of salmon and Pan-seared Hokkaido Scallops. The taste of foods are so delicious and the atmosphere of the place is also nice. The service team are also helpful and so friendly. Thank you all for lovely memory and I'm sure I'm coming back here again.

Outstanding steak, largest cheese board I have seen in years and a wine collection to die for
Beautifully located in an old house beside Inya lake, take aperitifs out on the lawn. Inside there is a nice lounge area with around 40 bottles of fine wines you can taste from 10cl to full glasses rather than just buy one. A basement wine cellar leads to a lovely table for 4 in the cooler cellar. After a wonderful steak, so tender, i elected for the cheese board - it was more of a table on wheels, perhaps 40 choices. It was a midweek and 7.30pm visit so quieter than usual according to my colleagues but for later meals better to book in advance
